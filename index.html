Ingredients

  • 1 small onion roughly chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast cut bite size
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons flour
  • 1/2 green bell pepper finely chopped
  • 1 medium potato peeled and roughly chopped
  • 3 medium carrots peeled and roughly chopped
  • 10 string beans sliced in half
  • 12 button mushrooms sliced in half
  • 3 cups low sodium chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup 2% milk
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Cream Stew

Description

Cream stew (クリームシチュー kurīmu shichū) is a popular Yōshoku dish consisting of meat, usually chicken or pork, and mixed vegetables, onion, carrot, potato and cabbage, cooked in thick white roux.

Directions

  1. In a pot over high heat, add oil, onions and garlic. Cook for 3 minutes.
  2. Add chicken and wine and cook for about 5 minutes, until chicken is cooked through.
  3. Lower heat to medium/high, add flour and stir for two minutes.
  4. Add sliced potatoes, bell pepper, carrots, string beans and mushrooms. Cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add chicken broth and bring to boil. Lower heat to a simmer, cover and cook for about 25 minutes, until vegetables are tender.
  6. Turn the heat off, add milk and butter and season with salt and pepper. Add more milk if the sauce is too thick. Serve warm.
